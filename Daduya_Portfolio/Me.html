ABOUT ME WHO AM I

I am Raphael J. Daduya, a Bachelor of Science in Information
Technology (BSIT) from City College of Calamba. I am
passionate about technology and how it can be used to solve
real problems and make everyday tasks easier. Throughout my
academic journey, I gained experience in system development,
database management, and user-centered design. I enjoy
creating solutions that are practical, reliable, and easy to use.
I am driven, adaptable, and always willing to learn new tools and
skills to improve my craft. Whether working independently or as
part of a team, I make sure to stay focused on the goal and
deliver quality work. My goal is to continue growing as an IT
professional and contribute to meaningful projects in the tech
industry.
